Common Inquiries

General Booking & Logistics

How far in advance should I book my session?

For large-scale events or weddings, we recommend booking 6 to 12 months in advance.

Do you travel for shoots?

Absolutely. We offer services worldwide.

What happens if it rains?

We offer a one-time complimentary reschedule or pivot to an indoor location.

Photography Services

How many photos will I receive?

Portrait/Headshot: 15–30 images. Full-Day Event: 50–75 images per hour.

What is your "retouching" policy?

Standard includes color and minor blemishes. High-end is an add-on.

Do I get the RAW files?

We do not provide RAW files. We deliver finished high-resolution JPEGs.

Videography Services

Highlight Reel vs Full Edit?

Highlight: 3–5 min cinematic edit. Full: Chronological cut of the event.

Can I choose the music?

We use licensed music but take your stylistic input into account.

How do you handle audio?

We use lapel mics and external recorders for crisp sound.

Delivery & Rights

What is the turnaround time?

Photography: 1–2 weeks. Videography: 4–6 weeks.

Who owns the copyright?

The studio retains copyright; you receive a Personal or Commercial Use License.
