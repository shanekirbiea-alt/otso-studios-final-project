Common Inquiries

General Booking & Logistics

How far in advance should I book my session?

For large-scale events or weddings, we recommend booking 6 to 12 months in advance. For corporate headshots or smaller lifestyle sessions, 2 to 4 weeks is usually sufficient. However, if you have a specific date in mind, it’s always best to reach out as early as possible.

Do you travel for shoots?

Absolutely. We offer services worldwide. Travel fees typically include airfare, accommodation, and a modest per diem. For local shoots within a 50-mile radius, travel is usually included in the package price.

What happens if it rains or there is bad weather?

For outdoor sessions, we monitor the forecast closely. If the weather is prohibitive, we offer a one-time complimentary reschedule to the next available date. Alternatively, we can pivot to an indoor studio location if preferred.

Photography Services

How many photos will I receive?

Quality always beats quantity. On average, you can expect:
• Portrait/Headshot Session: 15–30 fully retouched images.
• Full-Day Event: 50–75 OTSO STUDIOS images per hour of coverage.

What is your "retouching" policy?

Our standard editing includes color correction, exposure adjustment, and minor blemish removal. "High-end" retouching (e.g., body contouring, advanced skin smoothing, or object removal) is available as an add-on service.

Do I get the RAW files?

We do not provide RAW files. Much like a chef doesn't serve raw ingredients, our editing is a vital part of our artistic signature. You will receive high-resolution, professionally finished JPEGs.

Videography Services

What is the difference between a "Highlight Reel" and a "Full Edit"?

• Highlight Reel: A 3–5 minute cinematic "best-of" set to music, perfect for social media and sharing.
• Full Edit: A longer, chronological cut of the event (e.g., the full keynote speech or the entire wedding ceremony).

Can I choose the music for my video?

We love your input! However, for copyright reasons, we use high-quality licensed music libraries to ensure your video isn't flagged or removed from platforms like YouTube or Instagram. We will provide a curated selection of tracks for you to choose from.

How do you handle audio recording?

We use professional-grade lapel microphones and external recorders to capture "crisp" audio. For events, we often take a direct feed from the soundboard to ensure every word is heard perfectly without background noise.

Delivery & Rights

What is the turnaround time?

• Photography: 1–2 weeks for a full gallery.
• Videography: 4–6 weeks, depending on the complexity of the edit and motion graphics.
• Note: We often provide a "Sneak Peek" within 48 hours for social media use.

Who owns the copyright to the images and videos?

By standard practice, the creator (the studio) retains the copyright. However, you are granted a Personal Use License (for weddings/portraits) or a Commercial Usage License (for businesses) that allows you to print, share, and use the media for marketing purposes.
